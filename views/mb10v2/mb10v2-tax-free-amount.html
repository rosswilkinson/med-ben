Your tax free amount

6 April 2015 to 5 April 2016

Your tax free amount is
£10,200

Your tax free amount is spread across the tax year. Each payday you get part of your tax free amount so that by the end of the tax year you’ve had the full tax free amount available to you.

Everyone who lives in the UK receives some income before tax has to be paid - a “tax free amount” of income. That tax free amount is a “personal allowance” that depends on your circumstances.

How we work out your tax free amount

Tax free allowances Amount (£)
Personal Allowance 10,600
Less medical benefit 400
Total 10,200

Your tax free amount of £10,200 is used to create your tax code.
