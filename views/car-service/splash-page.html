Check or update your company car tax


Tell HMRC about changes to your company car that happen from 6 April 2015 to 5 April 2016.

Use this service to check your current car details, change your car details or update your fuel benefit.

You must verify your identity to use this service for the first time. You’ll need:

  • a valid UK passport or UK photocard driving licence
  • access to your financial information

You only have to verify your identity once - after that you can sign in with your username and password.

Before you start

You’ll also need:

  • the car’s list price (including VAT and accessories)
  • CO2 emissions information

You can get these details from the car manufacturer or your employer.

You can’t use this service if you’re part of a car averaging, car sharing scheme or your Employer is payrolling benefits.

Contact HMRC or your employer to update your company car details if you can’t do it online.
