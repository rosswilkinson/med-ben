Check or update your medical insurance beneift

Use this service to:

  • check the value of a benefit you’re currently getting
  • remove a benefit you’ve never had from an employer
  • report that you've stopped getting a benefit

If your benefit stopped on or after 6 April 2015, the employer that provided it will be able to confirm how much they paid.

You can't use this service to:

  • add new benefits or view any policy details
  • change the value of a benefit you still get from an employer
  • change the value of a benefit you had before 6 April 2015
