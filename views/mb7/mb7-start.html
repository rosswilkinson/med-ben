HM Revenue and Customs

 

Check or update medical insurance benefit details

Tell HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) about changes to your private medical insurance company benefit that happen between 6 April 2015 and 5 April 2016.

Use this service to:

  • check if you’re currently getting medical insurance benefit
  • tell HMRC that you’ve stopped getting medical insurance benefit
  • tell HMRC to remove medical insurance benefit that you’ve never had

You can’t use this service to:

  • add new benefits
  • change the value of benefits you still get from an employer
  • change the details of benefits you were getting before 6 April 2015

Before you start

If you stopped getting the benefit on or after 6 April 2015, you’ll need to ask your employer how much they’d paid for it from 6 April 2015 until the date it stopped.

You must verify your identity to use this service for the first time. You’ll need:

  • a valid UK passport or UK photocard driving licence
  • access to your financial information

You only have to verify your identity once, after that you can sign in with your username and password.
