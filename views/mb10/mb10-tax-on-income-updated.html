If your income looks wrong

It might be because:

  • tax free deductions have already been taken off (these are payments for things like a company pension or childcare vouchers)
  • bonus or overtime payments may be temporarily affecting your estimated annual income
  • our records need to be updated by your employer
You can update your income on our records.
